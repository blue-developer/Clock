Calendar — Events

Events from this calendar appear on the weekly calendar strip.
In Calendar.app: right-click a calendar → Share Calendar → enable Public Calendar → copy the link.

Calendar — Reminders

Events from this calendar trigger the full-screen reminder alert but do not appear on the weekly calendar strip.
Use a separate iCloud calendar dedicated to medication, bathroom, and exercise reminders.
Starts with webcal:// — paste it as-is.
